About Us

I am Gaurav Gupta, Director of Education in the School of Computing at Macquarie University. I hold a Master of Computing degree from the National University of Singapore (2004) and a PhD in Information Security from Macquarie University (2008). With 20 years of experience teaching Mathematics and Computer Science at the university level in Australia, I strongly believe in the importance of strong fundamentals and first principles for long-term success and genuine enjoyment of the subject.

At G2 Tutoring, we provide personalised tutoring for students in Years 5–10 of the Australian education system. Our focus is on building confidence and strong foundations in Mathematics and Computer Programming. We believe in interactive, engaging sessions that make learning fun and practical.

Subjects We Cover

  • Programming:

    • C++
