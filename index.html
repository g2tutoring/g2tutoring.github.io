About Us

I am Gaurav Gupta, Director of Education in the School of Computing at Macquarie University. I hold a Master of Computing degree from the National University of Singapore (2004) and a PhD in Information Security from Macquarie University (2008). With 20 years of experience teaching Mathematics and Computer Science at the university-level in Australia, I understand the skills school kids need to thrive in universities, and truly enjoy what they are doing. I strongly believe in the importance of strong fundamentals and first principles for long-term success and genuine enjoyment of the subject.

At G2 Tutoring, I provide personalised tutoring for students in Years 5–10. My aim is to get children excited about what they are studying, and we we academic success as a happy by-product.

Subjects Covered

  • Mathematics:
    • Numbers
      • Numeracy skills (fractions, decimals, percentages, integers)
      • Arithmetic operations
    • Algebra
      • Patterns and expressions
      • Linear equations and graphs
      • Quadratic equations
    • Measurement
      • Length, area, volume
      • Units and conversions
      • Time and rates
    • Space (Geometry)
      • Shapes and angles
      • Congruence and similarity
      • Pythagoras’ theorem
      • Trigonometry
    • Statistics
      • Data displays and interpretation
      • Measures of central tendency (mean, median, mode, range)
      • Scatterplots and correlation
    • Probability
      • Chance experiments
      • Independent and dependent events
      • Theoretical vs experimental probability
  • Programming:
    • Python: Variables and operators including Strings, conditions, loops, functions, lists, dictionaries, maps, classes and objects.
    • Java: Variables and operators including Strings, conditions, loops, functions, arrays, ArrayLists, classes and objects.
    • Processing (visual coding): Same topics as Java, but in a visual, animation-based environment. Suitable for years 5 - 7.
    • HTML: Basics of web development.
    • CSS: Styling web pages.
